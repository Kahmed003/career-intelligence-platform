# 1. Executive Summary

Career OS is an AI-powered operating system designed to help ambitious students and early-career professionals make better long-term career decisions.

Unlike traditional productivity tools, Career OS does not simply organize information or manage tasks. It continuously connects knowledge, opportunities, relationships, goals, and evidence to help users make better decisions throughout their professional lives.

Career OS serves three equally important purposes:

1. Organize professional information.
2. Support strategic decision-making.
3. Help users execute their highest-impact work every day.

---

# 2. Mission

> **Career OS exists to maximize the quality of a user's career decisions over a lifetime by combining knowledge, relationships, opportunities, and AI into a single reasoning system.**

---

# 3. Vision

Career OS becomes the trusted operating system that users rely on every morning to determine:

* What deserves attention today.
* Which opportunities should not be missed.
* Which decisions require thoughtful consideration.
* Whether they are making meaningful progress toward long-term goals.

# 6. Product Principles

## Principle 1

The AI never replaces the user's judgment.

It augments it.

---

## Principle 2

Every recommendation must be explainable.

Career OS always explains:

* Why now
* Why this recommendation
* Evidence used
* Tradeoffs
* Confidence
* Long-term impact

---

## Principle 3

Information should only be stored if it can later contribute to a better decision.

---

## Principle 4

The platform should optimize for long-term career outcomes rather than short-term productivity alone.

---

## Principle 5

Organization, strategic thinking, and execution are equally important.

# 13. Opportunity Definition

An Opportunity is any external event, position, relationship, or program that has the potential to advance one or more long-term goals.

Examples include:

* Internship
* Full-time role
* PhD program
* Fellowship
* Scholarship
* Research assistantship
* Conference
* Leadership program
* Mentor introduction

---

# 14. Project Definition

A Project is a deliberate investment of time, knowledge, and effort intended to create long-term career value while producing measurable outcomes.

Projects exist to build assets.

Examples:

* Skills
* Research profile
* Portfolio
* Professional network
* Technical credibility

---

# 15. Knowledge Definition

Career OS stores reusable knowledge rather than isolated notes.

Knowledge is linked across:

* People
* Projects
* Opportunities
* Research
* Decisions
* Goals

Knowledge is searchable, reusable, and continuously enriched by AI.

# 17. Long-Term Vision

Career OS evolves from a personal operating system into a scalable platform for ambitious students, researchers, and professionals.

Its defining capability is not task management.

Its defining capability is helping users consistently make better career decisions by integrating organization, knowledge, relationships, and intelligent reasoning into one coherent system.
